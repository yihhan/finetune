# Insurance Act

## Overview
The Insurance Act regulates insurance business in Singapore and establishes prudential requirements for insurers.

## Questions and Answers

Q: What does the Insurance Act regulate?
A: The Insurance Act regulates insurance business in Singapore, including licensing, prudential requirements, and consumer protection.

Q: What are the capital requirements for insurers?
A: Insurers must maintain minimum capital requirements based on their risk profile and business activities, as specified by MAS.
