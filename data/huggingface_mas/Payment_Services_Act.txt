# Payment Services Act

## Overview
The Payment Services Act regulates digital payment services and money-changing services in Singapore.

## Questions and Answers

Q: What does the Payment Services Act regulate?
A: The Act regulates digital payment services, money-changing services, and other payment-related activities in Singapore.

Q: What licenses are required for payment services?
A: Providers need money-changing, standard payment institution, or major payment institution licenses based on their business activities and transaction volumes.
