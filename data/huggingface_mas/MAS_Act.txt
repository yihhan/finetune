# MAS Act

## Overview
The Monetary Authority of Singapore Act establishes MAS as Singapore's central bank and integrated financial regulator.

## Questions and Answers

Q: What is the Monetary Authority of Singapore Act?
A: The MAS Act establishes MAS as Singapore's central bank and integrated financial regulator with powers to regulate and supervise financial institutions.

Q: What are MAS's main functions?
A: MAS's main functions include monetary policy, financial supervision, financial market development, and currency issuance.
